ARTIST DETAILS

Michael Ball

Michael Ashley Ball OBE is an English singer, presenter and actor. In 1985,
he made his West End debut as Marius Pontmercy in the original production of Les Misérables.

Naseeruddin Shah

Naseeruddin Shah is an Indian actor. He was notable in Indian, parallel cinema
and has starred in various international productions. He has won numerous awards in his career,
including three National Film Awards,three Filmfare Awards and the Volpi Cup for Best Actor
at the Venice Film Festival.

Pankaj Kapur

Pankaj Kapur is an Indian actor who has worked in Hindi theatre, television
and films. Recipient of a Filmfare Award and three National Film Awards, his most acclaimed film
roles include that of Inspector.

Ragnar Kjartansson

Ragnar Kjartansson is a contemporary Icelandic artist who engages multiple artistic
mediums, creating video installations, performances, drawings, and paintings that draw upon
myriad historical and cultural references.

Leah Clark

Leah Clark is an American voice actress, ADR director and script writer. She has provided voices
for Japanese anime series and video games.

Theatron Magazines





New Way To Dream

The New Way to Dream magazine inspires creativity and personal growth, exploring topics like dream interpretation, goal-setting, and self-improvement. It encourages readers to push boundaries and transform their dreams into reality.








Play Bill

A playbill is a program distributed at theater performances, featuring details about the cast, crew, and production. It often includes a synopsis, actor biographies, and acknowledgments. Playbills serve as both an informative guide and a keepsake for theatergoers.








Musicals

Musicals are theatrical performances that combine songs, spoken dialogue, and dance to tell a story. The music and lyrics are integral to the plot, enhancing emotional depth and advancing the narrative. Musicals are known for their lively performances, memorable songs, and captivating choreography








The Glow Of Success

The glow of success represents the sense of accomplishment and fulfillment that comes from achieving one’s goals. It’s the shining feeling of pride and confidence that follows hard work, perseverance, and overcoming obstacles. This glow serves as both motivation and a reminder of the power of determination and effort.
